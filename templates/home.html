Welcome to Scorecard

Your personal online scorecard that you can use whilst watching the Eurovision Song Contest.

Vote for your favourite acts during each live show and view the live scores from everyone using Scorecard.

Voting takes place throughout the show. You can vote at any time using the Vote tab.

Vote for every country. You can award 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 10 or 12 points to each country.

You can see an average of the points awarded to each entry on the Scores tab. The winner of this year's Scorecard home vote is the entry with the highest average points.
